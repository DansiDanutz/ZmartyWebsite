Understanding Win Rate Ratios: Long vs Short Positions

September 28, 2025 7 min read

Win Rate Ratios are one of Zmarty's most powerful features. Learn how we calculate them and how to use them to make better trading decisions.

What Are Win Rate Ratios?

A Win Rate Ratio shows the historical success probability of Long vs Short positions based on current market patterns. For example:

BTC/USD Current Pattern:

Long Position Win Rate: 68%

Short Position Win Rate: 32%

How We Calculate It

Zmarty analyzes thousands of historical instances where similar patterns occurred:

  1. Pattern Recognition - Identify current technical patterns
  2. Historical Matching - Find 1,000+ similar past occurrences
  3. Outcome Analysis - Track which positions profited
  4. Statistical Calculation - Calculate win percentages

Real Data, Not Guesses

Every Win Rate we show is based on verified historical data. We track:

  • Pattern completion rates
  • Price movement after pattern formation
  • Volume confirmation statistics
  • Market condition correlations

How to Use Win Rates

High Long Win Rate (>60%) - Historical data suggests long positions had higher success in similar conditions.

High Short Win Rate (>60%) - Past patterns indicate short positions performed better.

Balanced (40-60%) - Uncertain market, consider other factors.

Important Disclaimer

Win Rates are statistical probabilities based on historical data, not guarantees. Always combine with your own analysis and risk management.
